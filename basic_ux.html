This theme was focused on creating a prototype following the 5-day Design sprint method – from mapping, through sketching, deciding to prototyping. We were working in groups of 7 people this time and our job was to create an app that would improve student’s life for KEA students.

On the last day of sprint, we gave 7-minute clear pitch of our solution, where we introduced our team and included the problem and solution we came up with to the problem for our target audience.

During this assignment I was introduced to Design sprint method. I was able to work at a fast pace and coming with ideas using the Design sprint method which was very useful.

We were working in a team of 7 people where each of us had specific role. The roles were well distributed and decided at the beginning of the project. Facilitator led our team and decider (myself) helped with tough decisions. We made team canvas, where we decided on roles for each member and set goal, purpose, and rules and talked about individual strengths and weaknesses.
